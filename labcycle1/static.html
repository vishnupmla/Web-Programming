About Us

The College of Engineering Chengannur commonly known as CEC, is an engineering institute in the state of Kerala, India that was established by the Government of Kerala under the aegis of the Institute of Human Resources Development in 1993. The college is located in Chengannur,alappuzha.

Mission is to impart engineering education that enables the students to design, develop and maintain systems, products and process for the society utilizing the evolving technology. To orient the students for professional success, critical thinking and equity

  • inculcate stdents with entrepreneurial skills, ethical and social values
